Overnight Stay:

6 Nights

3 Cities

Sydney, Port Stephens, Hunter Valley

Season:

November 2023 - December 2023

Assemble at Singapore Changi Airport for your flight to Sydney, the state capital of New South Wales.

Highlights: Iris Lodge Alpacas, Port Stephens Koala Sanctuary

Upon arrival, head to Iris Lodge Alpacas whereby you will have the opportunity to get up close and hand feeding the cute alpacas. Thereafter, proceed to Port Stephens and check in at Port Stephens Koala Sanctuary - the four-star glamping tents are set within the sanctuary grounds, in the shade of beautiful tall trees.

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Highlights: Koala Sanctuary, Dolphin Watch Cruise, Stockton Sand Dunes, Irukandji Shark and Ray Encounter

After breakfast, join a guided tour at Koala Sanctuary. Thereafter, set out on a relaxing Dolphin Watch Cruise to spot the wild dolphins living in these waters and observe them socializing together in their environment. After lunch, venture off-road on an exciting fun-filled ride in a 4WD vehicle to explore the desert-like landscape and towering sand dunes which may reach as high as 40m at Stockton Sand Dunes. Bring along your swimwear and towels for a unique guided encounster which differs from the typical glass aquarium experience at Irukandji Shark and Ray Encounter where you will get to learn about marine conservation while getting in water to feed, and interact with different species of sharks and rays.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Highlights: Cookabarra Fish Farm, Hunter Wine Theatre

Today, visit the Cookabarra Fish Farm and join a guided farm and gardens tour to see how the award-winning Australian made technology operates to produce over a thousand kilograms of barramundi per week. After that, enjoy their famous Barramundi lunch with fresh Sydney Rock Oysters. Later, continue to visit Hunter Wine Theatre for a visual presentation on the history of Hunter Valley, view the wine-making process and enjoy a wine-tasting session.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Highlights: Hunter Valley Cheese and Chocolate Company, Birkenhead Point Outlet, Clearview Glassboat Dinner Cruise

After breakfast, head to Hunter Valley Cheese and Chocolate Company. Upon arrival in Sydney, visit Birkenhead Point Outlet, one of Sydney's most loved shopping destinations, providing quality shopping. This evening, board a Clearview Glassboat Dinner Cruise and enjoy the panoramic harbour views.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Highlights: Chinatown, St Mary’s Cathedral, Darling Harbour, Kings Cross, The Rocks, Sydney Opera House, Mrs Macquarie’s Chair, Sydney Harbour Bridge, Sydney Fish Market

This morning, go on a Sydney city tour to Chinatown, St Mary’s Cathedral, Darling Harbour, Kings Cross, The Rocks, Sydney Opera House, Mrs Macquarie’s Chair, and Sydney Harbour Bridge. After that, stop at Sydney Fish Market, the largest market of its kind in the Southern Hemisphere for lunch. Enjoy the rest of the day at leisure.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

If time permits, you can do some last–minute shopping before you transfer to the airport for your flight home. Note Travellers taking an early morning flight will be provided with a packed breakfast
